Construction Guide - Professional Build Process

📐 Step 1: Design & Templates

Create precision CAD templates for each cutout. Export as DXF files for CNC cutting or create paper templates for manual cutting.

📏 Step 2: Measurements

Measure all equipment precisely. Add 2-3mm clearance for easy insertion/removal. Account for cables and attachments.

🔍 Step 3: Foam Selection

Use high-quality Kaizen foam (charcoal color). Thickness: 10mm base + 20mm cutout layer + 5mm top layer.

⚙️ Step 4: CNC Cutting

Program CNC router with 3mm end mill. Cut at 800mm/min feed rate. Leave 0.5mm skin for clean edges.

🔗 Step 5: Assembly

Layer foam with spray adhesive. Align registration marks. Press firmly and cure for 24 hours.

✂️ Step 6: Finishing

Trim excess material. Sand rough edges. Apply protective coating to high-wear areas.

🧪 Step 7: Testing

Test fit all equipment. Adjust cutouts as needed. Verify easy access and secure fit.

📋 Step 8: Quality Control

Inspect for defects. Check foam adhesion. Label compartments. Create inventory system.

Material Requirements

Kaizen Foam Sheets: 3 sheets @ 24" × 16" × 35mm
